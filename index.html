PROJECT_01 / 02
🏆 Presented at VISAI 2026 — International

IoT Smart Fire Detection & Suppression System

ESP32 · LoRa · Multi-Sensor Fusion · Automated Response

The challenge: detect and suppress fires in remote, infrastructure-less environments where Wi-Fi doesn't exist and response time is measured in seconds. The solution required rethinking every layer of the stack — from sensor placement to communication architecture.

I designed a multi-node LoRa mesh capable of kilometer-range telemetry with sub-second sensor reporting. The system fuses flame, gas (MQ-series), and temperature data with redundant logic to eliminate false positives — then triggers servo-actuated suppression autonomously.

⚡ EV Drivetrain Engineering · Team Lead

E-Bike Motor Speed Controller

Arduino · PWM · BTS7960 · Hall Sensor · Battery Management

Electric vehicles live or die by their drivetrain efficiency. This project tasked me with designing a closed-loop PWM speed controller for an EV prototype — balancing performance, thermal safety, and rider responsiveness.

I led a 6-engineer team through PCB integration, firmware development, and stability testing. The final system integrated a Hall Effect throttle, BTS7960 H-bridge driver, real-time OLED telemetry, and overcurrent protection — a complete embedded drivetrain control unit.
